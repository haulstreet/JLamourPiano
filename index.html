About

Craftsmanship in
Every Key

Joseph L'Amour began his journey into piano technology in 2011 as an apprentice under Tim Coates, RPT — a Registered Piano Technician with over 50 years of experience. That rigorous foundation instilled a commitment to precision that defines every service he performs today.

From weekly concert preparation at the University of South Dakota to tuning historic instruments at the National Music Museum, Joseph brings equal care to every piano he touches — whether a concert Steinway or a century-old fortepiano.

He holds a Bachelor of Music Education (Vocal & Piano, USD, 2001) and has conducted private instruction since graduation — teaching piano, guitar, voice, music theory, and composition.

What Joseph
Can Do For You

Concert Tuning

Precision equal-temperament tuning to A440 (or custom pitch) for concert-ready performance. Available for rehearsals, recordings, and events.

🔧

Regulation & Voicing

Grand and upright action regulation, hammer voicing (reshaping, hardening, softening) to restore the touch and tone your instrument deserves.

Institutional Contracts

Ongoing maintenance agreements for schools, universities, and performance venues. Includes inventory management and scheduled service visits.
