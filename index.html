What I Offer

Expert Piano
Services

Every piano has a voice. My work is to help it speak clearly — whether it's a centuries-old fortepiano in a museum or a concert grand on a professional stage.

Concert Tuning
Pre-performance pitch calibration for concert grands — tuning for the South Dakota Symphony, touring ensembles, and festival performances.
Action Regulation
Comprehensive grand and upright action regulation — restoring factory-level touch response, evenness, and expressive control.
Hammer Voicing
Reshaping, needling, hardening and lacquering hammer felts to shape tonal color from bright and focused to warm and singing.
Repairs & Parts
Wire tying, string installation, damper pin regulation, pedal lyre repair, key bushing replacement, and general mechanical restoration.
Historic Keyboards
Specialist tuning and maintenance of harpsichords, 19th-century pianos, and fortepianos. Cornell-trained — serving the National Music Museum collection.
Cabinet & Finish
High-gloss polyester lacquer touch-up and wood grain restoration — bringing an instrument's exterior back to showroom condition.
Institutional Care
Ongoing maintenance contracts for universities, school districts, and concert venues — with detailed inventory management and scheduled tuning cycles.
Full Rebuilds
Complete instrument restoration including pinblock replacement, restringing, bridge work, and action rebuild — returning vintage instruments to full playing condition.
Private Instruction
Private lessons in piano, guitar, voice, music theory, and composition — for students of all ages, with performance opportunities through organized recitals.
